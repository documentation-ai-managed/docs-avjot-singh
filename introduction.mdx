## Overview

Avjot Singh's documentation space provides a centralized hub where you create, organize, and manage all your project documentation. You gain full control over structuring content with Markdown and MDX, enabling rich, interactive pages powered by modern components. This platform scales with your projects, from simple READMEs to comprehensive API references.

<Callout kind="info">
  Brand your space with custom colors like `#3B82F6` for a professional look.
</Callout>

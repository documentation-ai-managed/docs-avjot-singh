## Prerequisites

Before you begin, ensure you have the following ready:

<Callout kind="info">

- A modern web browser like Chrome, Firefox, or Safari
- An email address for account verification
- Optional: A GitHub account for version control integration

</Callout>

## Create Your Account and Log In

Follow these steps to set up your Avjot Singh account quickly.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Visit the Dashboard" icon="monitor">
    Open your browser and navigate to `https://dashboard.avjotsingh.com`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Sign Up" icon="user-plus">
    Click **Sign Up** and enter your email, name, and a strong password. Avjot Singh sends a verification email immediately.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify Email" icon="mail">
    Check your inbox and click the verification link. Return to the dashboard and log in with your credentials.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete Onboarding" icon="check-circle">
    Answer a few quick questions about your project to personalize your space.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Configure Your Initial Space

Customize your documentation space settings to match your project needs.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Basic Setup" icon="settings">
    In the dashboard, select **Space Settings** from the sidebar. Set your space name (e.g., "My Project Docs") and brand color to `#3B82F6`.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Integrations" icon="plug">
    Enable GitHub sync under **Integrations**. Connect your repository by pasting the repo URL, like `https://github.com/yourusername/my-project-docs`.
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Permissions" icon="shield">
    Invite team members via email. Assign roles: Admin, Editor, or Viewer.
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

<Callout kind="tip">
  Save changes after each tab. Your space updates in real-time.
</Callout>

## Create Your First Documentation Page

Build your first MDX page with live preview.

<Steps>
  <Step title="New Page" icon="plus">
    Click **New Page** in the top navigation. Name it "Introduction".
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add Content" icon="edit-3">
    Use the editor to write your page. Here's a starter MDX template:
    
    <CodeGroup tabs="MDX,Basics">
    ````mdx
    ---
    title: Introduction
    description: Welcome to my project docs.
    ---
    
    ## Overview
    
    This is your first page.
    ````
    
    ````mdx
    # Hello World (Markdown only)
    
    - List item 1
    - List item 2
    ````
    </CodeGroup>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Preview and Publish" icon="eye">
    Toggle the preview pane. Click **Publish** to make it live. Share the URL: `https://docs.avjotsingh.com/yourspace/introduction`.
  </Step>
</Steps>

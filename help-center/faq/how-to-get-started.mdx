## Overview

To begin using Avjot Singh's documentation platform:

1. Sign up at `https://docs.avjotsingh.com/register`
2. Verify your email
3. Log in to the dashboard

## Create Your First Project

- Click "New Project"
- Name your project and set permissions
- Start organizing your documentation!

Need help? Contact support anytime.

description: Fix access issues with Avjot Singh's docs.
---

## Symptoms

- Error: "Page not found" or "Access denied"
- Unable to view documentation pages

## Quick Fix

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log in again">
    ````
    Go to https://docs.avjotsingh.com/login and enter your credentials.
    ````
  </Step>
  <Step title="Check Permissions">
    Ensure you have project access in your team settings.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Clear Cache">
    Try clearing browser cache or using private mode.
  </Step>
</Steps>
